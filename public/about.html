About MoodCast

Features

  • Real-time weather data based on user location
  • Quick mood logging with six emotion options
  • Visual trends using interactive charts
  • Personal insights into correlations between weather and mood

APIs Used

  • Open-Meteo API: Free, real-time weather data
  • Chart.js: Interactive charts to display mood trends
  • Days.js: Converts dates to reader-friendly labels
  • Supabase: Backend for storing and fetching mood entries

Background

Research shows that weather conditions can influence mood, energy levels, and overall mental well-being. Changes in temperature, sunlight, and seasonal patterns have been linked to emotions such as happiness, stress, and fatigue. MoodCast was created to help users track their moods alongside real-time weather data in order to identify patterns over time. By visualizing these trends, users can gain greater awareness of how environmental factors may affect their emotional state.
